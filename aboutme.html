About Me

I am a dynamic and expressive individual with a passion for movement, creativity, and self discovery. My journey began on the soccer field, where I developed discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Alongside that, dance has become a powerful form of expression for allowing me to connect with music and tell stories through motion. At my core, I am someone who thrives on new experiences. I love exploring different perspectives, pushing boundaries, and constantly evolving into the best version of myself.
